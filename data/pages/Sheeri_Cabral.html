Sheeri Cabral (born 17 September 1978), née Kritzer, is a MySQL community contributor. She was chosen as the first[1] Oracle ACE Director for MySQL.[2] She won the MySQL Community Advocate, Communicator and Facilitator award in 2007[3] and again in 2008[4] for her frequent blog posts, community work, and conference/user group presentations;[5] this sparked a keynote presentation for the 2009 MySQL User Conference & Expo on "How to be a Community Superhero".[6] She delivered another community keynote entitled, "Under New Management: Next Steps for the Community" [7] at the same conference in 2010. In 2012, she won the MySQL Community Award again [8] for her work founding and co-hosting the OurSQL podcast.

Cabral wrote the MySQL Administrator's Bible,[9] and has edited the O'Reilly books SQL Hacks[10](2007), High Performance MySQL 2nd edition[11](2008), and C.J. Date's How to Write Accurate SQL Code.[12] Cabral developed a new version of mysqltuner[13] (with permission from Major Hayden for the name), which led to developing the mysql_health check Nagios plugin.[14]

Cabral has a master's degree in Computer Science from Brandeis University and is MySQL 5.0 DBA Certified and MySQL Core Certified.[15]

Other notable work[edit]

  • Program Committee Member for the Open Source Convention (OSCon) for 2011 and 2012[16]
  • IOUG MySQL Council Member, 2011–present[17]
  • MySQL Track Manager for Collaborate 2011[18]
  • Organizer of OpenSQLCamp Boston[19]
  • Program Committee Member for the 2010 MySQL User Conference & Expo[20]
  • Organizer of the 2009 MySQL Camp[21]
  • Boston MySQL User Group Founder and frequent speaker[22]
  • Technocation, Inc Founder and Treasurer 2004–2017[23]
  • Volunteer DBA work for The Human Creativity Project[24]
  • MySQL Podcast ("OurSQL"), November 2006 – February 2009, December 2010 – June 2015 (founder and co-host to over 200 episodes)[25][26]

Cabral has worked as a Systems Administrator for Tufts University; a Database Administrator for Guardium, Inc, Manhunt.net and The Pythian Group, a Database Operations Manager for PalominoDB, Inc., and a Senior Database Administrator/Architect, then Data Team Manager at Mozilla. She currently works as a Senior Database Administrator for Salesforce .
